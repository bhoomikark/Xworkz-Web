Different Breeds in Shih tzu

Imperial Shih Tzu

Imperial Shih Tzus are a smaller variation of the Shih Tzu breed, often referred to as "teacup" or "micro" Shih Tzus, and are known for their compact size and playful, affectionate personalities

American Shih Tzu

The American Kennel Club (AKC) recognizes Shih Tzus as a purebred dog breed since 1969, known for their long, silky fur, small size, and affectionate personalities, making them popular family pets.
